## Cross-reads

- **Six names disclosed NO new-build plans**: 6920 Lasertec, 3436 SUMCO, 6857 Advantest, 6315 TOWA, 6728 Ulvac, 4966 Uyemura. CIP / capex is going into **equipment-into-existing-shells**, not new construction. Asset-light models confirmed for Lasertec / Advantest. SUMCO's silence is striking — the Yoshinogawa pause shows up at the BS level (CIP −67% YoY) but the yuho doesn't disclose what comes next.
- **Kumamoto cluster (TSMC JASM gravity)**: TEL Kyushu Koshi-shi (¥43B R&D + ¥18B process-eval), JEM Kikuchi-shi (¥1.3B across 4 lines), JCU Mashiki (¥11.4B) — three names with active new-build in Kumamoto Prefecture, all feeding the JASM ecosystem. **JEM's entire probecard pipeline is Kumamoto + Sanda — every single line item.**
- **Gifu cluster = Ibiden alone** (¥298B disclosed across Kawama + Ogaki + Ohno) — single-firm cluster underlining the CoWoS-substrate moat. Will expand to ¥500B in the FY26 yuho (June 2026) on the May 2026 board approval.
- **Disclosed plans far smaller than BS CIP — suggests off-yuho pipelines**: Advantest (zero disclosed, ¥6B CIP), SUMCO (zero disclosed, ¥123B CIP), TOWA (zero disclosed, ¥0.8B CIP but recently announced), Renesas (¥85B aggregate but ¥131B CIP — Kofu reopening not broken out).
- **Only 6 explicit hard capacity numbers disclosed**: TEL Miyagi (+250%), TEL Solutions Iwate (+50%), Shinko Chikuma (+50%), Shinko Arai (BGA cap expansion), Stella Chemifa Sanpo (~2× filling), Kanto Denka Mizushima (新設・増強). The vast majority hide behind 算定が困難 boilerplate.
- **Korea sub-cluster**: 6871 Micronics Bucheon (¥4B), 8035 TEL Korea Hwaseong (¥62.5B), 4186 TOK Pyeongtaek (¥12B) — multiple JP filers building Korea capacity to serve SK Hynix + Samsung memory.
