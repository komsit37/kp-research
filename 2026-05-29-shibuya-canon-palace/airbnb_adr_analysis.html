7 · Conclusion & caveats

Corrected for the size-parsing bug and classified by bedroom count, the studio host ADR is ≈¥26,338/night (≈22% above the original ¥21,545, below the buggy ¥28,126), lifting minpaku-legal @50%-occ net yield to ~3.31% vs 2.87% for long-term rent. STR remains the higher-yield strategy on these numbers; the 2BR-remodel idea is not supported on ADR.
Caveats: (1) asking ≠ realised — get an AirDNA RevPAR report; (2) only 28/80 listings state size, so comp sizing leans on bedroom count; (3) per-window n is small (studio 3–9); (4) 22 units competing ≠ one listing; (5) minpaku 180-night (~49%) cap is the hard ceiling.
